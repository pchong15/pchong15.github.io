New System

Creating a Better Application Process

  • May 2017

Summary

For one of my classes at school, I was put on a team to re-evaluate a soccer league's system. We put together a new system by identifying problems using the PIECES Framework (Performance, Information, Economics, Control, Efficiency, and Service). The outcome –a website that could track applicants and streamline the payment process to create an easy user experience for the clients.

The Problem

We built out their current system, creating an ERD/DFD to find out that the company is having trouble tracking all their clients. The owner was using an Excel sheet and physical papers to sign up clients, which led to a huge drop off.

Customers did not fill out the form properly, leaving fields empty; consequently, the owner had to track people down to get information. Simply put, he needed a better system.

We did user research and interview current clients, the results were clear that they wanted the sign-up process to be easier. Many stated that if it wasn't for the coaching staff, they would have went elsewhere. We identfied that clients wanted a way to pay.
